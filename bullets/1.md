This writeup talks about a client side DoS vuln. found on Google Keep. The DoS occured due to unicode characters. So an attacker creates notes with multiple unicode 
characters and when he/she shares the notes to a victim email; their Google Keep account will crash. Author said it was a surprise he got paid for DoS.
